About Santhosh Narayanan

Santhosh made his debut as an independent music director in the 2012 Tamil film Attakathi directed by Pa. Ranjith. He got the opportunity through its producer C. V. Kumar who saw him working in his studio. Santhosh composed a song in the Gaana genre for the film, "Aadi Pona Aavani".

He worked with Maajja on independent music as Enjoy Enjami sung by Arivu, Dhee and himself which clocked 19 million views in one week of release making it the highest watched Tamil music video album and keeping now at 474 million views. Neeye Oli also featured in Sarpatta Parambarai as the theme song.
