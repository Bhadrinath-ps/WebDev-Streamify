G. V. Prakash Kumar

An Indian music composer, playback singer, actor and film producer in Tamil and Telugu films. His first film was Veyil and he gained fame in Tamil films by the early 2010s and made his debut on 2015 Darling.

Won one National Award and three Filmfare Awards in his film career.

About G. V. Prakash Kumar

He first appeared as a vocalist of director S. Shankar's Tamil film Gentleman (1993), composed by his maternal uncle, A. R. Rahman. He has also contributed to some of Rahman's other projects also worked with Harris Jayaraj and sang two songs in Anniyan (2005) and Unnale Unnale (2007).

G.V. Prakash's introduction as a film composer was in the critically acclaimed Tamil film Veyil (2006) directed by Vasanthabalan. Hismusic in the film Madrasapattinam (2010), directed by A. L. Vijay was appreciated, especially his song "Pookal Pookum Tharunam".
